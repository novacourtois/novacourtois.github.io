Play Fair

PlayFair

Project Description

One-page app that makes collaborative Spotify playlists fair by normalizing the playlist to account for how many songs individuals put in, and how long they are.

It's still not completely done, but we used a locally-hosted version at my previous work and everybody loved it. Built with AngularJS, Node.JS, and the Spotify API.
